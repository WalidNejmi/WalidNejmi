<h1 align="center">Hi 👋, I'm Walid Nejmi</h1>
<h3 align="center">A passionate Software Engineer from Morocco 🇲🇦</h3>

I'm a software engineer with a passion for building cool stuff and solving real-world problems with code. I was born and raised in Casablanca, Morocco 🇲🇦, and now I’m studying Computer Science and Philosophy at Tufts University in Boston 🐘. Along the way, I’ve studied abroad in Italy 🇮🇹 and will soon be studying in Hong Kong 🇭🇰—I love exploring new places and stepping out of my comfort zone!<br><br>

Right now, I’m a Software Engineer Intern at Homebase, where I’m building responsive, user-friendly websites using Next.js, Sanity, and AWS. I enjoy everything from designing the front-end to integrating back-end APIs. I'm also passionate about backend development and have worked on several projects using C/C++, including creating image processing tools, developing simulation systems, and building file compression programs. Whether it’s crafting efficient algorithms for large-scale data or designing programs that are performant, I love the challenges that come with backend engineering! 🧠

In my free time, you'll probably find me playing basketball 🏀, reading a good book 📚, or thinking about new projects to build. I'm always excited to learn something new, whether it's a programming language, a tool, or a fresh perspective on solving problems!<br><br>
